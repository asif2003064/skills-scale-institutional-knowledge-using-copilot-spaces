# Role Onboarding Checklist

This checklist helps new team members onboard into their role on OctoAcme projects. Use this template to ensure consistent onboarding and knowledge transfer.

---

## General Onboarding (All Roles)

- [ ] Review OctoAcme Project Management Overview ([docs/octoacme-project-management-overview.md](./octoacme-project-management-overview.md))
- [ ] Review Roles and Personas document ([docs/octoacme-roles-and-personas.md](./octoacme-roles-and-personas.md))
- [ ] Understand your role's responsibilities and goals
- [ ] Identify key stakeholders and collaboration points with other roles
- [ ] Set up access to project management tools (boards, repositories, communication channels)
- [ ] Attend project kickoff or orientation meeting
- [ ] Review current project charter or one-pager
- [ ] Review communication cadence and meeting schedules
- [ ] Understand escalation paths and decision-making processes

---

## Role-Specific Onboarding

### Developers
- [ ] Set up development environment and access to repositories
- [ ] Review coding standards and contribution guidelines
- [ ] Understand branching strategy and CI/CD pipeline
- [ ] Review current sprint backlog and upcoming work
- [ ] Pair with experienced developer on first tasks
- [ ] Understand testing requirements and code review process
- [ ] Review technical architecture and design documents

### Product Managers
- [ ] Review product vision, roadmap, and OKRs
- [ ] Understand customer personas and user research
- [ ] Review backlog prioritization criteria and framework
- [ ] Meet key stakeholders (customers, leadership, partners)
- [ ] Understand data analytics and metrics tracking
- [ ] Review competitive landscape and market positioning
- [ ] Align on product strategy with leadership

### Project Managers
- [ ] Review project plans, timelines, and milestones
- [ ] Understand risk register and current risks/dependencies
- [ ] Access project tracking tools and dashboards
- [ ] Review communication plan and stakeholder map
- [ ] Understand resource allocation and team capacity
- [ ] Review meeting cadence and facilitation responsibilities
- [ ] Align on reporting requirements with stakeholders

### Business Analysts
- [ ] Review current requirements documentation and backlogs
- [ ] Understand stakeholder landscape and business context
- [ ] Access to requirements management tools
- [ ] Review business process documentation
- [ ] Understand acceptance criteria standards
- [ ] Shadow experienced BA in stakeholder interviews
- [ ] Review requirements traceability practices

### UX Designers
- [ ] Review design system and style guides
- [ ] Access design tools (Figma, Sketch, etc.)
- [ ] Review user research findings and personas
- [ ] Understand accessibility requirements and standards
- [ ] Review current design backlog and priorities
- [ ] Meet with Product and Development teams
- [ ] Understand design review and feedback process

### QA Lead
- [ ] Review test strategy and quality standards
- [ ] Access to test management and defect tracking tools
- [ ] Understand test automation framework and coverage
- [ ] Review current quality metrics and dashboards
- [ ] Understand release process and deployment criteria
- [ ] Review defect triage process and SLAs
- [ ] Meet with Development and Release teams

### Agile Coach
- [ ] Review current team agile maturity and practices
- [ ] Understand organizational agile framework
- [ ] Observe current agile ceremonies
- [ ] Review team health metrics and retrospective notes
- [ ] Meet with team members individually
- [ ] Understand coaching goals and success criteria
- [ ] Review process improvement backlog

### Executive Sponsor
- [ ] Review business case and strategic objectives
- [ ] Understand budget, resources, and constraints
- [ ] Review stakeholder map and governance structure
- [ ] Align on success metrics and KPIs
- [ ] Understand escalation process and decision rights
- [ ] Review current project status and risks
- [ ] Schedule regular steering committee meetings

---

## First 30 Days

- [ ] Week 1: Focus on understanding role, team, and project context
- [ ] Week 2: Begin contributing to team activities and deliverables
- [ ] Week 3: Take ownership of initial work items or initiatives
- [ ] Week 4: Conduct 30-day retrospective with manager to identify support needs

---

## Continuous Learning

- [ ] Participate in retrospectives and incorporate feedback
- [ ] Identify areas for skill development
- [ ] Share knowledge with team members
- [ ] Contribute to process improvements
- [ ] Stay updated on best practices in your domain
